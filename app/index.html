The Awkwardly True Story

Over six years ago, Aki & Mark were leading very different lives on very different paths. By chance, they both found themselves working the same position within Apple… and their first encounter involved Aki managing to screw Mark’s inventory up from 95 miles away. After a phone call to clear it all up, Mark told his coworker “She sounds cute”.

After meeting a few times, Aki asked Mark if he wanted to go on an adventure. Mark asked Aki if she wanted to be his girlfriend. Only one said yes.

Mark was patient though. Eventually she told him, in a fit of defeat frustration, “I want you to be my !@#$%^ boyfriend!” and then they left for Europe.

The rest, as they say, is history, and now, six years later, we’re finally getting married!
